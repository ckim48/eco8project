Data Science

The Data Science team played a crucial role in the Eco8 capstone program, contributing to the project’s objective of sustainable development and environmental protection on Jeju Island. Upon collection and analysis of review data from application users, develop analysis tools, create dashboards, and visualize user data to understand behavior patterns and make informed decisions for improvements. Aimed to provide data-driven insights that support the project’s sustainable development goals and enhance the overall effectiveness of the application.

For this project, our task was to improve the design of an application aimed to digitize a physical ‘Wind Map’, a popular tool used by residents of Jeju Island for engaging in activities supporting sustainability goals.
